About me.

Before commencing study at Coder Academy, I worked for over 10 years in the music industry. As a pianist and musical directory I have had the privilege to perform on some of the best stages in Australia and across the globe.

I have worked tirelessly running my own business, building relationships with clients and actively engaging with the Australian music community. Whilst I still maintain a busy performance schedule, my focus is now on helping other businesses improve their online presence.

When I'm not touring or building websites, I enjoy learning and exploring all things Japan! After a 2019 trip to the land of the rising sun I was inspired to learn the Japanese language as well as cook my favourite dishes, including Ramen and Sushi!
