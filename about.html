About me.

Before commencing study at Coder Academy, I worked for over 10 years in the music industry.

As a pianist and musical directory I have had the privilege to perform on some of the best stages in Australia and across the globe. I have worked tirelessly running my own business, building relationships and actively engaging with the Australian music community. Whilst still maintaining a busy performance schedule, my focus is now on helping other businesses reach their potential.

When I'm not touring or building websites, I enjoy learning and exploring all things Japan! After a 2019 trip to the land of the rising sun I was inspired to learn the Japanese language as well as cook my favourite dishes, including Ramen and Sushi!
