About me.

Before commencing study at Coder Academy, I worked for over 10 years in the music industry. As a pianist and musical director I have had the privilege to perform on some of the best stages in Australia and across the globe.

In addition to performing I've enjoyed sharing my skills with others, tutoring in schools, online and at my private studio. Whilst I still maintain a busy performance schedule, my focus is now on helping other artists and creatives improve their online presence.

When I'm not touring or building websites, I enjoy learning and exploring all things Japan! After a 2019 trip to the land of the rising sun I was inspired to learn the Japanese language as well as cook my favourite dishes, including Ramen and Sushi!
